Impact Factor vs. JUFO - Software Engineering Forums

Federation of Finnish Learned Societies has ranked all peer reviewed publication forums for scientists in Julkaisufoorumi (JUFO). The ranking has three levels 1 = basic; 2 = leading; 3 = top.   The purpose of this page is to compare how the ranking compares with various other measures. The list of forums is limited on ISI-indexed software engineering journals listed by Robert Feldt with the addition of all SE forums ranked to level 2 or 3 (Robert==TRUE || Publication_Forum_Ranking>=2). In case I have missed any forums let me know.

In the table :

  • IF = Impact Factor (ISI web of science journal citation reports),
  • JSS = The journal is included in the Journal of Systems and Software annual ranking of top software engineering scholars (publisher's site, open access).
  • JUFO = Julkaisufoorumi (1 = basic; 2 = leading; 3 = top)
  • GS_H5 = Google Scholar H5 index
