Work

Starting 8/2014 I work as an assistant professor in the department of CSE.

In 2012-2014 I worked as a senior research scientist and a teacher in software engineering at Aalto University.  My work was funded by a post-doc grant of department of CSE and an EU FP7 project called U-QASAR.

In 2011-2012, I was a post-doc in Software Engineering Research Group at Lund University, Sweden. The work was funded by the ELLIIT project.

In 2010/1-2010/4, I was a guest researcher at Simula Research Laboratory, Norway.

In 2008-2011, I was a project-manager of ESPA research project at Aalto University.

In 2003-2008, my work was funded by SHAPE and SEMS research projects as well as SoSE graduate school.
