Impact Factor vs. Publication Forum - Comparison for software engineering scholars in Finland 

Federation of Finnish Learned Societies has ranked all peer reviewed publication forums for scientist. The ranking has three levels 1 = basic; 2 = leading; 3 = top.   The purpose of this page is to compare how the ranking of the forums compares with impact factor rankings ranking from ISI-web of science. The list of forums is limited on ISI-indexed software engineering journals listed by Robert Feldt with the addition of all SE forums ranked level 2 or 3. In terms of computer logic it is: (Robert==TRUE || Ranking>=2). In case I have missed any forums send an email.

 

In the table :

  • IF = Impact Factor (ISI web of science journal citation reports),
  • JSS = The journal is included in the Journal of Systems and Software annual ranking of top software engineering scholars (publisher site, open access).
  • PF = Publication Forum (1 = basic; 2 = leading; 3 = top)

Notes from the data

TSE is by all measures ranked as the top journal in Software Engineering

SPE is the "low hanging fruit" with low impact factor but inclusion in the JSS list and ranked as leading (2) journal in PF list.

TOSEM is the only other journal ranked as a top (3) in addition to TSE in the publication forum list. However, there are four other journals that have higher impact factor than TOSEM, but are still ranked as "only" leading (2) journals.

Conferences are not highly valued in publication forum list. Only ICSE and ESEC/FSE ranked as leading forums.
