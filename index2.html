Work

I'm a researcher in the Software Process Research Group (SPRG). In my research I'm developing approaches to improve software maintainability (or design quality if you accept the following viewpoint to software design) with software processes, human evaluations and machine learning. I have two instructors Casper Lassenius and Timo Honkela. Few past years I have been working in the SEMS research project. Now I'm working in a new  research project called SHAPE.
