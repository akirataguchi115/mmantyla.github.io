Doctoral Seminar M3S (Information Processing Science), 2021

Date 11 June, 2021

Time 09-16 (depdending on the number of participants)

Location: Online

Submission deadline: 28 May, 2021 (EEST)
Review deadline: 4 June, 2021

Completing the required steps gives a student 1 ECTS required to complete (Research Plan & Seminar (Part two 920004J-02)).

Requirements

  1. Submit a dissertation research plan (part A of Doctoral training plan) following UNIOGS guidelines. All submission should be written in English:

    "Your paper should clearly state:

    • The problem to be solved in your thesis (justify why this problem is important and make clear that previous research and related work has not yet solved that problem),
    • Your research objectives,
    • The expected contributions of your dissertation research, including discussion with related work
    • How you plan to evaluate your results and present credible evidence of your results to the community."

  2. Review 3 research plans assigned to you. Provide all reviews according to the following template:
    • Paper summary
    • Points for the paper
    • Points against the paper
    • Support your points
    • Suggested paper improvements

  3. Attend the doctoral seminar day (video watching required)
  4. Present your research plan at the doctoral seminar day (video required)
  5. Receive feedback of your plan (discussion on Zoom)
  6. Discuss and provide feedback to all the authors whose plan you reviewed

Submission are done via EasyChair (Link will be updated).

No separate registration required, i.e. completing step 1. in the above will register you to the seminar.

 

Program

Each presentation is 10-15 minutes (video) + 20 minutes discussion as separate.
